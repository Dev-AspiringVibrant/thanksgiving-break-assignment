Country's Own Thanksgiving Food

Each countries has their variety of food to feast on! In variation, it does not have to be turkey because it can be replaced by another dish.

Mexico

In Mexico, Pumpkin Empanadas is one of their traditional dish. Most Mexican dish have pumpkin as their main ingredient. When not in season, many cooks uses canned pumpkin to make pumpkin empanadas. However, during the Thanksgiving festival, they cook with pumpkins that are fresh.

Cuba

Cuba's Thanksgiving dish, Beans and Rice or congri, is one of the most easiest dish to cook. Another upside is the dish is inexpensive to make. Compared to most Thanksgiving dishes, it is one of the healthiest dish to eat. During Thanksgiving, they are stirred together when cooking rather than separately. Some may prefer brown rice for healthier substitution.

Sweden

Hasselback Potatoes is a Sweden traditional dish where the potato is cut in layers without cutting through the other end to look more of accordion-shape. They have to be baked more than a little usual to get the crispy flavor. Its last step is to top them with buttery bread cumbs and parmesan cheese.

Ethiopia

Ethiopia's Thanksgiving dish is Doro Wat.
